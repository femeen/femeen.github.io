Feminism...

Feminism is the belief that all people should be free from unequal treatment based on their characteristics(gender,race, religion, disbilities,etc.). Feminist strve for social, political, and economic equality, as well as oppurtunities for all type of people.

Do you understand Feminism?

Feminism is the equality of men and women. Many people believe that feminism is the belief that women should be above men. Many people are against feminism because they dont know what feminism is. They believe that feminist try to belittle men and make women above men socially, politically and economically. However, feminism is a movement for equality between all types of people(mainly gender). Feminism benefit men and women in many different ways. One example is that a feminist goal is to eliminate gender stereotypes for both men and women. Women don't need to be perfect delicate flowers and men don't have to be strong and brave all of the time. All in all feminism has many different meanings that many people don't fully understand.
